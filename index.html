About Me

Hey, I’m Mady, a singer, songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, and lifelong music lover. I’ve never been one to stick to a single sound or scene, and honestly, that’s what keeps music exciting for me.

Music has always been how I explore the world. I’ve ventured into a variety of musical spaces, from musical theater and opera to jazz, rock, and indie projects. I’m a vocalist first, but I’ll pick up any instrument I can get my hands on, including bass, drums, keys, and guitar. Each one teaches me something new about how music connects us, how rhythm moves people, and how melody can tell a story words alone cannot always convey.

These days I’m based in Sacramento, teaching and performing full time. I love helping students grow into fearless, well-rounded musicians who stay curious, push themselves, and find their own sound. My goal is to make music feel approachable for everyone, no matter where you’re starting or what style lights you up.

When I’m not teaching, you’ll find me performing, writing, and collaborating with musicians around Sacramento and beyond. I’m drawn to projects that mix genres, blur edges, and chase emotion over perfection. I like when things feel a little unpredictable and alive.

At the heart of it all, I believe music is a lifelong conversation, one that deepens every time you share it with someone new. So if you’re here to listen, collaborate, or just see what I’m creating, welcome. I’m glad you’re here.
